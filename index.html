Selection Process of The Prime League

  1. The players who perform well in TPL will get selected for state league.
  2. Every state will have a team in TPL and there will be 3 matches of every team.
  3. The player who perform best in state level will get selected for national level.
  4. The batsman who will make 100 runs in 3 state level matches of TPL will be selected for auction
  5. The bowler who take 9 wickets in 3 state level matches of TPL will be selected for auction
  6. The all - rounder who will make 75 runs and take 5 wickets will get selected of auction
  7. The wicket keeper who will make 80 runs and involved in 6 wickets, then he will be selected for auction
  8. 120 players will be selected for 8 final matches of TPL and there will be 15 players in each team
  9. Auction price is from ( Rs. 5000 - Rs.200000 ) rupees
  10. Man of the match for each natonal level match is Rs.1000 and a trophy
  11. The final winner team get Rs. 500000 cash prize and a trophy
  12. The runner - up team get Rs. 250000 cash prize and a trophy
  13. The best batsman wil get Rs. 50000 cash prize and a trophy
  14. The best bowler will get Rs. 50000 cash prize and trophy
  15. The man of the match in TPL final match is Rs. 21000 and trophy
  16. The man of the series for the national level is Rs. 100000 and trophy
  17. Free academy coaching will be provided to best 11 players of TPL and and expenditure will be made by TPL
